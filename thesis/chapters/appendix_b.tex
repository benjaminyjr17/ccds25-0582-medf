\chapter{Framework YAML Definitions}
\label{app:yaml}

This appendix reproduces the YAML configuration files that define the three governance frameworks integrated into MEDF. These files are located in the \texttt{app/frameworks/} directory of the repository.

\section{EU ALTAI (\texttt{eu\_altai.yaml})}

\begin{lstlisting}[language={}, caption={EU ALTAI framework definition (excerpt).}]
id: eu_altai
name: EU Assessment List for Trustworthy AI
version: "1.0"
source: AI HLEG, European Commission, 2020
dimensions:
  transparency:
    name: Transparency and Explainability
    weight: 0.18
    criteria:
      - id: T1
        text: Traceability of AI decisions
      - id: T2
        text: Explainability of model outputs
      - id: T3
        text: Communication of limitations
  fairness:
    name: Fairness and Non-discrimination
    weight: 0.20
    criteria:
      - id: F1
        text: Avoidance of unfair bias
      - id: F2
        text: Accessibility and universal design
      - id: F3
        text: Stakeholder participation
  safety:
    name: Safety and Robustness
    weight: 0.18
    criteria:
      - id: S1
        text: Resilience to attack
      - id: S2
        text: Fallback plan and safety
      - id: S3
        text: Accuracy and reliability
  privacy:
    name: Privacy and Data Governance
    weight: 0.18
    criteria:
      - id: P1
        text: Privacy and data protection
      - id: P2
        text: Quality and integrity of data
      - id: P3
        text: Access to data
  human_oversight:
    name: Human Agency and Oversight
    weight: 0.12

      - id: H1
        text: Human agency and autonomy
      - id: H2
        text: Human oversight mechanisms
  accountability:
    name: Accountability
    weight: 0.14

      - id: A1
        text: Auditability
      - id: A2
        text: Risk management
\end{lstlisting}

\section{NIST AI RMF (\texttt{nist\_ai\_rmf.yaml})}

The NIST framework definition follows the same structure, with 15 criteria mapped to the six unified dimensions. The weights reflect the emphasis of the NIST AI RMF's four core functions (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage) on each dimension.

\section{Singapore MGAF (\texttt{sg\_mgaf.yaml})}

The Singapore MGAF definition contains 12 criteria with weights reflecting the framework's emphasis on accountability and internal governance structures. The complete YAML files are available in the project repository.
